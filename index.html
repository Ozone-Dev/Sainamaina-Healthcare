    Cardiology

    Our team of skilled cardiologists is dedicated to delivering personalized care, addressing a wide range of cardiac issues with a patient-centered approach. From preventive measures to advanced interventions, we are committed to your heart health.

    Neurology

    Our neurology department offers comprehensive services for the diagnosis and treatment of neurological disorders and diseases. We are committed to providing expert care to enhance neurological health and well-being.

    With a focus on advanced diagnostics and treatment modalities, our neurology team addresses a spectrum of neurological conditions. From routine consultations to specialized procedures, we prioritize your neurological health and quality of life.

    Our hepatology department specializes in the diagnosis, treatment, and ongoing management of liver-related conditions. We are dedicated to providing comprehensive care for liver health and well-being.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Explore common questions about our hospital services and offerings.

    • What services does the hospital offer?

      Our hospital provides a wide range of medical services, including but not limited to general healthcare, specialized treatments, surgeries, and emergency care.

    • How can I schedule an appointment?

      To schedule an appointment, you can call our hospital reception or use our online appointment booking system on our official website.

    • Are there specialized doctors available?

      Yes, we have a team of specialized doctors in various fields such as cardiology, neurology, hepatology, pediatrics, and more. You can choose a doctor based on your specific medical needs.

    • What payment methods are accepted?

      We accept various payment methods, including cash, credit/debit cards, and online payments. Please check with our billing department for more details on accepted payment options.

    • How can I access my medical records?

      You can request access to your medical records through our patient portal on the website. Alternatively, you can contact our medical records department for assistance.

    • Is there parking available at the hospital?

      Yes, we have dedicated parking facilities for patients and visitors. Our parking areas are conveniently located near the hospital entrance.
